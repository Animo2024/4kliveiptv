## Pro Tips for the Best IPTV Experience

These tips will help you maintain peak performance on your **Z11 Pro Max IPTV** setup.

- **Always use Gigabit Ethernet** — even if your internet speed is "only" 100 Mbps, wired is infinitely more consistent than wireless for live IPTV streams.
- **Enable 4K HDR output** — go to Settings → Display → Resolution and set it to 4K 60Hz with HDR enabled. Match this to your TV's capabilities.
- **Enable hardware decoding in MyTV Online 3** — in the app's playback settings, ensure Hardware Decoder is toggled on. The Z11 Pro Max's dedicated decoder handles 4K HEVC streams far better than software decoding.
- **Keep MyTV Online 3 updated** — Formuler releases regular firmware and app updates. Enable automatic updates so you always have the latest performance improvements.
- **Use USB 3.0 storage for recordings** — connect a fast USB 3.0 drive for recording. USB 3.0 on the Z11 Pro Max delivers read/write speeds that support simultaneous recording and playback.
- **Contact support for MAG portal setup** — if you prefer to connect via MAG/Stalker portal mode, contact our support team after subscribing and we will provide your Portal URL.
